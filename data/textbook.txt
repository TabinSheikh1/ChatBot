Football History

- Origin: Modern football rules were codified in England in 1863.
- First International Match: Scotland vs. England, 1872.
- FIFA Founded: 1904 in Paris.
- World Cup: The first FIFA World Cup was held in 1930 in Uruguay.
- Evolution: Substitutions introduced in 1958 World Cup. Yellow and red cards introduced in 1970 World Cup.
- Famous Players: Pele, Diego Maradona, Lionel Messi, Cristiano Ronaldo.
- Football Today: Played professionally in over 200 countries, with billions of fans worldwide.
